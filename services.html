Energy Efficiency

Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirty solar panels can lose up to 25% of their energy output. In Hawaii's humid climate, panels accumulate dust, pollen, bird droppings, and mineral deposits faster than nearly anywhere else in the country.

Our eco-friendly solar panel cleaning removes all buildup using deionized, purified water that leaves zero residue. No detergents, no abrasives. Maximum sunlight absorption and restored efficiency.

Solar Panel Service Includes

  • Purified deionized water rinse
  • Soft-brush cleaning only
  • Debris and bird dropping removal
  • Mineral deposit treatment
  • Panel inspection for visible damage
  • Safe for all major panel brands

Property Protection

Gutter Cleaning & Flushing

Clogged gutters are one of the leading causes of water damage to homes and commercial buildings in Hawaii. Overflow from blocked gutters soaks into fascia boards, causes foundation erosion, and leads to mold growth in walls.

We clear every inch of your gutters by hand, then flush the entire system to confirm proper drainage. You'll receive a full condition report, including any damage we notice along the way.

Gutter Service Includes

  • Full debris removal by hand
  • Downspout clearing and flushing
  • System flow test
  • Condition report after completion
  • Safe for all gutter materials
  • Debris bagged and removed
