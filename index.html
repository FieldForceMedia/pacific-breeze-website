Our Process

The Pacific Breeze Method

A four-point cleaning protocol built for Hawaii's conditions.

01

Assess

We inspect your property first: mineral deposits, screen damage, track buildup, and problem areas specific to your location and exposure.

02

Deep Clean

Screens off and cleaned. Tracks vacuumed and wiped. Frames detailed. Glass scrubbed and squeegeed, interior and exterior, with eco-safe streak-free solution.

03

Detail

Final edge wipe on every pane. Frames touched up. Screens reinstalled and checked. We treat your home the way we'd want ours treated.

04

Verify

Full walkthrough before we leave. One streak = we fix it before we go. That's the streak-free guarantee. Every job.
