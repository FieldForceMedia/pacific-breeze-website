Our Story

We Started Because O'ahu Deserved Better

Pacific Breeze was founded with one belief: homeowners and business owners on O'ahu deserve a window cleaning company that cares about the quality of its work. A team that shows up on time, does the job right, and stands behind every window.

We're a local O'ahu company. We live here, work here, and we take pride in keeping Hawaii's homes and businesses looking their best. We use eco-friendly products because this island is our home too, and we treat every property like it's our own.

Today, Pacific Breeze holds a 5.0-star rating built one satisfied customer at a time. We're the most careful window cleaning company on the island.
